BERP (Bikloo's Enterprise Resoruce Management) is an ERP tool for the small scale businesses for managing their assets. This app for now is in development phase and is only focusing on the concept of selling products at stores. This app will provide the expensive softwares used at cash points in supermarts at low cost for stores and small scale busniesses. The idea is to implement all the ERP features but for the project submission we are focusing on cash points only.

Features of BERP

  • Control of Any Number of Businesses You Have.
  • Track the employees' work of each business.
  • Get the income and average monthly income of your business.
  • Add The Products
  • Easy Sign Up

How to Use (for Owners) ?

  1. Login or Signup as an owner of an enterprise.
  2. Add the enterprise listings you have (one or many).
  3. Add products which you're selling or the services you're offering under products.
  4. Add Employees working with you. Once you add, your individual employee will receive his login information at given email address.
  5. Track the business and employee's growth

How to Use (for Employees) ?

  1. Login to BERP.
  2. You'll see at which location you're working and will only be serving for that enterprise only.
  3. You'll be giving 10 points initially for any order you make and will be awarding for the points.
  4. The more points you earn the more awards you will be awarded by your owner.

*The awards may vary to your enterprise.
